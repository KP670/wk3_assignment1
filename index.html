Kristian's Must Try Restaurants

Traveling abroad give us the perfect opportunity to have a novel experience with our tastebuds. Here are my best places to dine here on Saipan.

Best Places to Dine:

  1. Citrine
  2. Chá
  3. Surf's Club

Citrine Cafe | Koberville, Saipan

Citrine offers great food to start the day. Whenever you have cravings for egg and rice and want to combine a bit of something in the morning, try their breakfast menu. From porksilog and spamsilog to tapsilog, anything that satisfy one's craving the morning.

What I love about Citrine:

  • Relaxing Atmosphere
  • The food
  • Iced tea

Chá Cafe | Garapan, Saipan

Chá is great place to go when you want to get up in the morning or to grab a sweet treat.

What I love about Chá:

  • The Macarons
  • The shakes and milktea
  • The croissants

Surf's Club | Chalan Kanoa, Saipan

Surf's Club is great place to go for a fulfilling dinner. Offering dishes like their juicy steaks and their delicious fried noodles.

What I love about Surf's Club:

  • The Steak.
  • Near a calm beach.
  • The crispy fried noodles.
